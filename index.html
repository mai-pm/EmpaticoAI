2. The opportunity
⚠ The problem
Empático's AI works — but the product looks like a prototype built by engineers with no designer in the room. Enterprise buyers need to trust what they see before they buy what they can't yet see.
✓ What we solve
We give Empático the identity, the interface, and the story it needs to sell. A brand that signals serious B2B product. A UI that feels inevitable — built for how CX teams actually think.
WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE
01
A product that earns attention
Walking into Zendesk Conference with something polished and compelling enough to stop the right people.
02
Design backed by real insight
Every decision informed by actual users — research that shapes the product, not just validates it.
03
A brand built to scale
An identity strong enough to grow — from first demo to first funding round to first 100 customers.
04
A UI that feels inevitable
So clear that users don't think about it — they just get answers. The product disappears; the insight remains.
05
Aerolab as a true partner
Not a vendor delivering specs — embedded in the problem, moving fast, thinking strategically.

01
Startup mode
Fast, scrappy, opinionated. No big reveals — WIP early and often. Short feedback loops.
02
Show, don't talk
Put pixels in front of Santi. Don't pitch in the abstract. Prototypes beat explanations every time.
