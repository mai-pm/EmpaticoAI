THE PRODUCT
What is Empático?

"AI virtual employees that live inside your CRM and answer questions about customer experience — via chat. No dashboards, no SQL, no weekly reports."

How it works
Connects to Zendesk → learns your brand context → creates AI analysts you can query conversationally
What it replaces
Manual tagging, spreadsheets, waiting for the weekly CX report, pinging the analyst for a quick answer
Current state
2 virtual employees live (CX Auditing + Trends). Design partners actively using it. AI is real.

CONTEXT
The opportunity
& our ambition
⚠ The problem
Empático's AI is real and working — but the product looks like what it is: a prototype built by engineers with no designer in the room. Enterprise buyers need to trust what they see before they buy what they can't yet see.
✓ What we solve
We give Empático the identity, the interface, and the story it needs to sell. A brand that signals serious B2B product. A website that generates demos. A UI that feels inevitable — simple, focused, built for how CX teams actually think.
WHAT SUCCESS LOOKS LIKE
01
A product that earns attention
Walking into Zendesk Conference with something polished, coherent, and compelling enough to stop the right people.
02
Design backed by real insight
Every decision informed by what we learn from actual users — research that shapes the product, not just validates it.
03
A brand built to scale
An identity strong enough to grow with the company — from first demo to first funding round to first 100 customers.
04
A UI that feels inevitable
An interface so clear that users don't think about it — they just get answers. The product disappears; the insight remains.
05
Aerolab as a true partner
Not a vendor delivering specs — a team embedded in the problem, moving fast, thinking strategically, raising the bar at every step.

HOW WE WORK
Mindset &
principles
01
Startup mode
Fast, scrappy, opinionated. No big reveals — WIP early and often. Short feedback loops.
02
Show, don't talk
Put pixels in front of Santi. Don't pitch in the abstract. Prototypes beat explanations.
03
Iterative by design
Brand first (wks 1–2). Website + product parallel from wk 3. Build → react → rebuild.
04
User insight built-in
Join Utoppia design partner sessions. Observe and fold insights into the design.
05
Time is real
Zendesk Conference is a hard date. Commercial readiness is the KPI for every decision we make.
06
Figma for handoff
We design in Figma, they implement. Website on Framer. UI kit delivered for their devs.
